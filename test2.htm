DETAILS OF EQUIPMENT REMOVAL
The contractor will be contacted by the identified user, as per Contract Part 7A, 7.7 "as when required" to remove all or requested police/safety/additional equipment without damage to the equipment, vehicle and manufacturers wiring in various vehicle platforms. In addition to the removal of the police/safety equipment platform specific factory equipment will need to be re-enabled (e.g. daytime running lights, lighting, horn, secure idle removal).A pre-inspection check sheet will be provided by the RCMP to assist with the location of all police/safety equipment for removal. All equipment must be returned to the identified user, as per Contract Part 7 A 7.7, in an organized fashion.
